# Echo #YourScript

## Who are we?

We are a team of developers from the [Clarity](https://github.com/Clarity-Studio) group, passionate about building open-source tools and creating high-quality software for the community.

Our goal is to develop reliable, maintainable, and developer-friendly projects while encouraging collaboration and knowledge sharing. We believe that great software is built together, and we welcome contributions, feedback, and ideas from everyone.

# Informations

> [!NOTE]
> Have an idea for a new source code or feature? Open a ticket on our [Official Discord](https://discord.gg/RfPJrmx6cf) and let us know.

<br>

> [!TIP]
> Contributions are welcome! Whether it's a bug fix, a new feature, or an improvement, feel free to open a pull request or reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/RfPJrmx6cf).

<br>

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This organization was created to host our open-source projects and collaborate with other developers. Note that **Clarity** remains our main project — join the [Clarity Discord](https://discord.gg/Pja39jyqkM) to learn more.

<br>

> [!WARNING]
> All official Echo scripts are published exclusively on our [GitHub](https://github.com/Echo-YourScripts) and shared through our [Discord](https://discord.gg/RfPJrmx6cf). If you find a repository claiming to be ours but not owned by a verified organization member, do not use it.

<br>

> [!CAUTION]
> Do not run scripts or executables claiming to be from Echo if they were not downloaded directly from our official [GitHub](https://github.com/Echo-YourScripts) or [Discord](https://discord.gg/RfPJrmx6cf). Modified or repackaged versions may contain malicious code and are not maintained or supported by us.
